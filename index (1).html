The Photographer

Joyantee
Raina

As words never felt enough, Joyantee Raina eventually found herself as a photographer. Before turning to photography, she completed her education in Women & Gender Studies.

She intends to understand how larger political forces shape individuals' lives at a very personal level & influence the ways people perceive & negotiate their identities in relation to the external world.

Joyantee is a loyal admirer of music and is fascinated by the most ordinary & mundane things. She sees the visuals she makes as a collective expression of her lived experiences.
